ABOUT VINDRU

We are a new-generation engineering company

VINDRU, established in 2025 in Coimbatore, India, is a new-generation engineering company committed to delivering smart, sustainable, and cost-effective drilling solutions. With a foundation built on over 15 years of hands-on industry experience, we specialize in products that serve the real needs of drilling professionals—across mining, water wells, exploration, and infrastructure.


We bring together field-tested engineering, technology-enabled design, and a customer-first mindset to solve critical challenges in fluid recovery, drilling efficiency, equipment reliability, and operational transparency.
